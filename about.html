Our Story

From a small bean to your daily cup.

Established 2026

The Cozy Bean began as a humble dream in a small garage. We wanted to create more than just a place to get caffeine; we wanted to build a sanctuary. A place where every cup tells a story of ethical sourcing, meticulous roasting, and passionate brewing.

Today, we are proud to serve our community with beans sourced directly from farmers we know by name, roasted right here in Visakhapatnam.

Our Values

Sustainability

We use 100% compostable packaging and support eco-friendly farming practices.

Quality

We only source the top 1% of Arabica beans, ensuring a superior taste profile.

Community

We believe in giving back. A portion of our profits goes to local charities.
