Signature Espresso Roast

$18.00

A dark, bold roast with notes of chocolate and caramel. Perfect for your morning espresso shot or a rich latte.

  • 100% Arabica Beans
  • Ethically Sourced
  • Roasted Fresh Weekly

Our Signature Espresso Roast is the heart of The Cozy Bean. We spent months perfecting this blend to ensure it stands up beautifully to milk while being smooth enough to enjoy on its own. It features beans from Brazil and Colombia, roasted to a full city dark level.

Expect a heavy body, low acidity, and a lingering sweet finish.

For Espresso: Use a fine grind. Dose 18g of coffee for a 36g yield in 25-30 seconds.

For Moka Pot: Use a medium-fine grind. Fill the basket loosely without tamping.

100% Whole Bean Arabica Coffee.

Allergens: None. Processed in a facility that also handles nuts.
